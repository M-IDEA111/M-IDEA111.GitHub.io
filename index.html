What is M IDEA?

M IDEA develops technologies that help people communicate, find communities, and grow businesses. Founded in 2025, the platform aims to empower individuals and creators through innovative video editing tools.

M IDEA offers integrated solutions that enable users to create stunning visual content with ease, while M IDEA Book provides a rich digital library of books and novels catering to diverse interests. We believe that creativity and knowledge are the foundation of progress and development in our digital world.
